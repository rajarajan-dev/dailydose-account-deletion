Account Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to rajarajan.abathsagayam@gmail.com.
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your account username or email address.
    • A request to delete your account and data.

Once we receive your request, we will process it within 7 business days. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at the same email address.

Note: Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes as required by law.
